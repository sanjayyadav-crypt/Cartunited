Process involved in Squirrelcart To Shopify Migration project

Planning and Organizing

First we understand the business objective of the client and then document all the key requirements and identify the key bottle-neck areas in the project. Based on our experience we establish a plan that ensures a smooth migration process and simplify the overall process and shorten the project timeline.

Theme Selection

We use out of box Shopify template that reflects the branded eCommerce experience. We even check for device compatibility. Based on the performance of your store in terms of conversion rates, trust-worthiness and more we customize a mobile-friendly theme for the new Shopify store. We ensure that the organization brand image through the website gets a boost.

Prepare The SEO Plans

As a part of the migration, we ensure SEO juice is retained including URLs, meta tags, meta titles, page titles, etc. For that, we compile a list of all the URLs and then post-migration we check all those URLs are retained and check for any broken URLs and pages. We make sure all the relevant pages are accessible to the search engines.

Data Migration

Actual migration of all the contents including the products, customer details, order details, categories, images, blogs, coupon codes, reviews, etc. are migrated to the new store. This step also involves the refactoring code third party extensions. We ensure that the entire content of the old store is migrated to the new store.

Reviews And Cleanup

Nothing gets delivered to our customers without performing testing at user acceptance and integration levels. This step of migration includes thorough testing of third party API and payment gateway integrations. Test orders are placed and end-to-end workflows are mocked for real-time testing.

Go-Live

After the development and testing activities cease, a final content validation is performed and voila! The final website is ready to go live over the internet.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I migrate from BASE to Shopify? Expand

While both platforms offer robust e-commerce solutions, Shopify provides a more user-friendly interface, greater flexibility, and an extensive range of apps and integrations. Additionally, Shopify's ecosystem is continually growing, offering you access to the latest features and innovations.

Will my data be safe during the migration process? Expand

Absolutely! Data security is a top priority for us. We utilize secure and reliable migration methods to ensure your data remains protected throughout the migration process.

How long does the migration process take? Expand

The duration of the migration process can vary depending on the size and complexity of your store. However, we strive to complete migrations as quickly as possible while ensuring a thorough and error-free transfer of data.

Will there be any downtime during the migration? Expand

We understand the importance of minimizing downtime for your business. Our migration process is designed to keep your store operational throughout the transition, ensuring minimal disruption to your customers' shopping experience.

Can you help with customizing my new Shopify store? Expand

Absolutely! We offer customization services to tailor your new Shopify store according to your branding and design preferences. Our team can assist with theme selection, layout customization, and integration of additional features to provide a unique and branded online shopping experience.

What support do you provide after the migration is complete? Expand

Our support doesn't end with the migration. We provide ongoing support to address any questions or concerns you may have as you settle into your new Shopify store. Our team is here to assist you in maximizing the potential of your new platform.

What about customization on my BASE store? Would you migrate them too? Expand

When migrating from platforms like Intershop to Shopify, our migration services primarily focus on transferring essential data such as products, customers, orders, and inventory. Customizations specific to the original platform may not be directly transferable due to differences in the underlying systems. However, our team can assist you in re-creating or implementing similar customizations on your new Shopify store. We work closely with you to understand your customization requirements and provide tailored solutions to match your branding and functionality needs.

My project is quite complex. How does your project management work? Expand

We assign a project manager to your account as your primary contact. This person will coordinate the necessary team members to execute on design and development projects, they will report progress to you on a schedule you both agree on, get your feedback and ultimately lead the project forward. Your project manager is also there to make strategic suggestions based on your specific business goals. Our goal is to take ownership and accountability of the process from start to finish.

How to make sure SEO remains entact after the migration & I don't lose organic traffic. Expand

Preserving SEO and minimizing any negative impact on organic traffic is a crucial aspect of our migration process. Here's how we ensure a smooth transition while maintaining SEO:

URL Mapping: We carefully map the URLs from your original store to the corresponding URLs on Shopify, ensuring that each page has a new URL structure that retains the relevance and hierarchy of your original website. This helps preserve SEO value and ensures proper redirection from old URLs to new ones.

301 Redirects: We implement 301 redirects from the old URLs to the new URLs on Shopify. These redirects inform search engines that the content has permanently moved, preserving the SEO authority of the original URLs and ensuring a seamless user experience.

Meta Tags and Content: We pay attention to preserving important meta tags such as page titles, meta descriptions, headings, and relevant content during the migration. This helps maintain consistency and SEO relevance for search engine indexing.

XML Sitemap: We generate a new XML sitemap for your Shopify store, which informs search engines about the updated structure and URLs of your site. This ensures that search engines can easily discover and index your new store's content.

Google Search Console: We assist you in verifying your new Shopify store in Google Search Console and submit the updated XML sitemap. This allows you to monitor the indexing status, fix any crawling issues, and ensure a smooth transition for organic traffic.
