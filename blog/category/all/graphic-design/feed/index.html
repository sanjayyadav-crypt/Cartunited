Banner Ads

Promote your latest collections, special offers, and events using banner ads.

What we offer for a single small task is:

  • If you provide us with some inspiration design example and assets, we can create up to 3 variations, one size.
  • If you provide us with some inspiration design example without assets, we can create up to 2 variations, one size.
  • If none of the above is provided, we can create 1 variation, one size.

If you need your banner ad in multiple sizes, after the design is approved, a new task is required in order to modify it to fit other sizes, and we can cover up to 5 different sizes of one design in a single task

(If a design requires some promotional message, some motto, or anything similar in terms of content-writing, it has to be provided by the client, as coming up with it is outside the scope.)

Basic Photo Manipulation

How many times you’ve found the almost perfect image for your Shopify store, but there’s something you’d like to be changed? I bet the answer is too many times.

We’ll do our best to make the perfect image from the almost perfect one.

Includes:

  • Cropping
  • Resizing
  • Small cosmetic adjustments
  • Removing elements from the images
  • Removing background from the images
  • Combining elements from multiple images into one
  • In one task, we can take care of up to 3 photos.

Please, keep in mind that the amount of work which can be done within 1-hour time-frame highly depends on numerous factors such as image resolution, image quality, image complexity, and the complexity of the request itself. You can always reach out to our design team, and we’ll do our best to provide you with the most precise assessment of your request.

Flyer And Brochure Design

What we offer for a single small task:

  • If you provide us with some inspiration design example and assets, we can create up to 2 versions, one page, two-sided.
  • If you provide us with some inspiration design example without assets, we can create 1 version, one page, two-sided.
